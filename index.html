MADE Club at GSMST!

"Implement by Increments"

MADE (Modeling Advanced Directional Engineering) strives to give aspiring engineers the following:

  • Real world application within the Big 5 Disciplines: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Computer and Chemical
  • Helping local communities and businesses with ideating and building products to help them grow!
